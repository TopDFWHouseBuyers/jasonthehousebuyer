Understanding the Probate Process in Crosby

Before you can sell an inherited property in Crosby, you typically need to navigate the probate process in Harris County. This legal procedure validates the will and transfers ownership to heirs. In Texas, probate can take several months depending on the estate complexity. Independent administration is the fastest option, while dependent administration requires court approval for most decisions. Working with a probate attorney familiar with Harris County procedures helps streamline the process. Once you have legal authority to sell, you can move forward with listing or accepting a cash offer.

Understanding Houston Code Violations

Houston operates under strict building and safety codes to protect residents. Common violations include structural issues, electrical problems, plumbing defects, roof damage, foundation cracks, unpermitted additions, and overgrown yards. The City of Houston can issue citations that come with fines and deadlines for compliance. When selling a house with code violations in Houston, many homeowners assume they must fix everything before listing, but that is not always the case.

Your Options When Selling With Code Violations

Second, you could disclose violations and sell at a reduced price to a traditional buyer willing to handle repairs themselves. Third, you could sell to a cash home buyer who purchases properties as-is, regardless of condition or violations.
